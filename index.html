Oxygen Magic Academy

Independent Research Initiative Exploring Human-Centered AI Governance, Complex Systems, and Future Education Frameworks

Research Foundation

Oxygen Magic Academy explores the role of human judgment as a structural interface within high-complexity technological environments. The research examines how decision stability, governance frameworks, and human cognitive boundaries interact in AI-mediated systems.

Research Reference: Tu, X. (2026). Human Judgment as an Irreducible Interface in High-Complexity Systems. Zenodo. DOI:10.5281/zenodo.18573944

Human-Centered AI Governance Framework

This research framework investigates how complex systems governance, AI infrastructures, and decision architectures interact through human judgment as a stabilizing structural layer. The approach integrates interdisciplinary perspectives including complex systems theory, governance design, and human cognitive constraints.

System Architecture

The Frequency Sovereignty System architecture provides a structural model connecting research frameworks, decentralized identity systems, and distributed knowledge infrastructures. This architecture integrates research publications, identity anchoring systems, and open research repositories to maintain transparency and continuity.

Research Mission

Oxygen Magic Academy aims to develop new frameworks for education, governance, and human-AI collaboration in emerging technological environments. The initiative explores long-term societal transformation including workforce transition, decision-making stability, and human cognitive resilience in AI-integrated systems.
